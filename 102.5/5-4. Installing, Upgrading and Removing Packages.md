### Install 
`$ zypper in unrar`

`$ zypper in /home/john/newpackage.rpm`

### Update all packages
`$ zypper update`

To only list the the available updates, without installing anything:
`$ zypper list-updates`

### Remove a package
`$ zypper rm unrar`

**Removing a package also removes any other packages that depend on it.**
